## üéØ Universal Debrief Prompt (Copy-Paste Ready)

```markdown
Please create a structured debrief of our conversation.

## INSTRUCTIONS

Generate a comprehensive but scannable summary with these sections:

### 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
- 3-5 bullet points covering:
  - What was the main topic/project?
  - What key insights emerged?
  - What decisions were made?

### 2. KEY INSIGHTS
List the 3-5 most important ideas/discoveries from our conversation.
Include brief context for each.

### 3. DECISIONS MADE
For each major decision:
- What did we decide?
- What options did we consider?
- Why did we choose this approach?
- What are the trade-offs?

### 4. ACTION ITEMS (Prioritized)
Organize next steps by priority:
- **HIGH PRIORITY:** Must-do items (time-sensitive, critical)
- **MEDIUM PRIORITY:** Should-do items (important but not urgent)
- **LOW PRIORITY:** Nice-to-have items (valuable but optional)

For each item include:
- What needs to be done?
- Who is responsible? (if applicable)
- What are the dependencies?

### 5. OPEN QUESTIONS
- What still needs research or clarification?
- What dependencies or unknowns remain?
- What assumptions did we make that need validation?

### 6. USEFUL ARTIFACTS
List any code, templates, frameworks, or content we created:
- Name and type of artifact
- Brief description of what it does
- Where it's located (if applicable)

### 7. RISKS & CONSIDERATIONS
- What could go wrong?
- What mitigation strategies did we identify?
- What constraints or limitations exist?

### 8. NEXT SESSION STARTER
Write a 2-3 sentence summary I can paste into a future chat to continue this project seamlessly.

Include:
- What we accomplished
- Where we left off
- What to focus on next

## OUTPUT FORMAT

- Use markdown formatting (headers, bullets, bold)
- Keep language concise and scannable
- Avoid dense paragraphs - use bullet points
- Be specific, not vague
- Include enough context that I'll understand this 3 weeks from now

Generate the debrief now.
```

## üìñ How to Use This Prompt

### Step 1: Have Your AI Session
Work with your AI tool (Claude, ChatGPT, etc.) on whatever project/question you have. Nothing changes in your normal workflow.

### Step 2: At the End of the Session
When you've completed your conversation and have your answers/artifacts/decisions, copy the entire prompt above (the part between the code blocks).

### Step 3: Paste and Run
Paste the prompt into your AI chat and press Enter. The AI will generate a structured debrief of your entire conversation.

### Step 4: Save the Output
Copy the AI's debrief response and save it to a file:
- Suggested naming: `debrief-YYYY-MM-DD-project-name.md`
- Example: `debrief-2025-11-16-api-architecture.md`

### Step 5: Reference Later
When you need to remember what you decided or continue the project later, read the debrief instead of re-reading the entire conversation.

**Time to run debrief:** 2 minutes
**Time saved later:** 15-30 minutes every time you reference it

## ‚ö° Quick Q&A Debrief (For Shorter Sessions)

For quick technical questions or short sessions under 2,000 words:

```markdown
Please create a quick reference summary:

1. **QUESTION:** What did I ask?
2. **ANSWER:** What was the solution/recommendation?
3. **KEY POINTS:** 2-3 most important things to remember
4. **CODE/ARTIFACTS:** Any code snippets or examples (if applicable)
5. **FOLLOW-UP:** What I should explore next (if applicable)

Keep it concise and scannable.
```

**Use this for:**
- "How do I configure X?"
- "What's the difference between Y and Z?"
- "Help me debug this error"
- Quick technical lookups
