## THE OPPORTUNITY

The Debrief Machine solves a real problem for a real market:

**Problem:** AI power users (Claude Pro, ChatGPT Plus subscribers) have long, brilliant conversations but can't remember the key insights, decisions, or next steps.

**Market:** ~500K Claude Pro + ChatGPT Plus users actively looking for session management solutions.

**Solution:** A collection of 3 core prompts that turn 10,000-word conversations into organized briefings in 2-5 minutes.

**Business Model:** Free lite version → Email list → $29 paid version → Potential expansion to product suite.

---

## THE STRATEGY IN 3 PARTS

### PART 1: FREE LITE VERSION (Lead Magnet)
**What:** 3 powerful prompts that work standalone
- Universal Session Debrief (works all platforms)
- Claude Project Edition (optimized for Claude)
- ADHD-Friendly Guide (brain-first structure)

**Why Free:** Validates market exists before investing in full productization

**Expected ROI:**
- Month 1: 300-750 downloads, 5-20 paid sales
- Month 1 Revenue: $145-$580
- Email List: 200-600 subscribers
- Cost: $0 (free to build)

### PART 2: PAID FULL VERSION ($29)
**What:** Everything in free + platform variants + advanced features
- 30+ prompts (vs 3 in free)
- ChatGPT + Perplexity editions (only Claude in free)
- Auto-trigger detection
- 27-section comprehensive briefings (vs 8-10 in free)
- Enterprise templates, specialized variants, etc.

**Why Paid:** Monetizes engaged users, funds business, incentivizes upgrade

**Expected ROI:**
- Month 1: 5-20 sales ($145-$580)
- Month 2-3: 10-30 sales/month ($290-$870)
- Month 4-12: 15-25 sales/month ($435-$725)
- Year 1 Total: $3,000-$6,000

### PART 3: VALIDATION METRICS
**Does market want this?**
- ✅ 50+ free downloads in 48 hours
- ✅ 25%+ email open rate
- ✅ 2%+ conversion to paid version
- ✅ 3-5 positive testimonials

**Red flags:**
- ❌ <20 downloads week 1 (no market interest)
- ❌ <5% email opens (messaging wrong)
- ❌ 0 paid sales (pricing/positioning issue)

## RISK MITIGATION

### Risk 1: "No one wants this"
**Indicator:** <20 downloads week 1
**Action:** Survey downloaders: "Would you pay? Why not?"
**Pivot:** Different market segment (researchers vs power users?)

### Risk 2: "Messaging is wrong"
**Indicator:** <5% email open rate on "paid version" email
**Action:** Test different email subject lines, value props
**Pivot:** Reposition as "research tool" vs "productivity tool"?

### Risk 3: "Price is too high"
**Indicator:** 0 paid sales after 100 free downloads
**Action:** Test lower price ($19), add "why it's worth $29" explainer
**Pivot:** Bundle with other products, create payment plan?

### Risk 4: "Market is too small"
**Indicator:** Plateau at <100 downloads/month
**Action:** Expand to secondary markets (researchers, ADHD professionals)
**Pivot:** Create specialized variants (Research edition, Legal edition)

**None of these risks are catastrophic** because the upfront investment is only 25 hours and $0.
