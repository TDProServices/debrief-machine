## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is this exactly?**
A: A collection of text prompts you copy-paste into AI chat interfaces. The AI reads the prompt and generates a structured summary of your conversation. Think of it as a framework for making AI conversations more organized and reusable.

**Q: Does this work with my AI tool?**
A: Yes. Works with Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and any AI that can follow structured instructions. Platform-specific editions are optimized for each tool's unique features, but the universal prompts work everywhere.

**Q: Do I need special software?**
A: No. This is just text prompts. Copy-paste into your AI chat interface. No installation, no plugins, no technical setup required.

**Q: How long does the debrief take?**
A: 2-3 minutes. Copy prompt, paste into chat, AI generates debrief, save output. That's it.

**Q: Is this worth it for short AI sessions?**
A: Probably not. Debriefs are most valuable for sessions over 2,000 words where you made decisions or created artifacts you'll need later. Quick Q&A sessions don't need formal debriefs.

**Q: What makes this different from just "taking notes"?**
A: The AI does the work. Instead of manually summarizing an 8,000-word conversation (executive dysfunction says no), you paste a prompt and the AI structures the summary for you. It's the difference between "I should organize this" and "It's already organized."
