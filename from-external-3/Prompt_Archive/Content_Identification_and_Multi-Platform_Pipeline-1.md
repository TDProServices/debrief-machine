## üéØ **CONTENT SCORING & PRIORITIZATION SYSTEM**

### **Automated Content Scoring Matrix**

```markdown
CONTENT PRIORITY ALGORITHM:

**VIRAL POTENTIAL (1-10 scale)**
- Surprise factor: How unexpected is the outcome?
- Relatability: Will audience see themselves in this?
- Shareability: Does it make people want to tell others?
- Visual appeal: Can it be demonstrated/shown?

**EDUCATIONAL VALUE (1-10 scale)**  
- Actionability: Can viewers immediately apply this?
- Completeness: Is the solution comprehensive?
- Uniqueness: Is this a fresh take or approach?
- Results: Are the outcomes clearly measurable?

**BRAND ALIGNMENT (1-10 scale)**
- Authority building: Does this establish expertise?
- Personality showcase: Does it reveal your unique voice?
- Value demonstration: Does it prove your worth?
- Community building: Will it attract your ideal audience?

**FINAL CONTENT SCORE**: (Viral + Educational + Brand) / 3
- **8-10**: Immediate production priority
- **6-7**: Schedule within 2 weeks  
- **4-5**: Add to content bank for later
- **1-3**: Archive unless strategic timing
```
