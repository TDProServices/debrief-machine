## üéØ **USAGE STRATEGY FOR COMPLEX CONTENT**

### **For Simple Projects/Conversations:**
Use **v2.3.0 single-phase** approach - immediate numbered briefing

### **For Complex Projects/Conversations:**
Use **v2.4.0 multi-phase** approach:

1. **Start with Phase 1** for immediate clarity and decision-making
2. **Use Phase 2** when you need comprehensive documentation
3. **Use Phase 3** when ready to move from analysis to action

### **ADHD-Friendly Benefits:**
- **Progressive disclosure** - get what you need when you need it
- **Cognitive load management** - no overwhelming walls of text
- **Action-oriented focus** - clear next steps at every phase
- **Scannable structure** - quick visual hierarchy for information location

---

**This solves the "too dense vs. too shallow" problem by giving you the right level of detail when you need it!**
