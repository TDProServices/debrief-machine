## Voice & Tone Guidelines

### Overall Voice

**We are:**
- Empathetic (we understand the pain)
- Practical (solution-focused, not hype)
- Respectful (users are smart, don't condescend)

**We are NOT:**
- Salesy (no aggressive tactics)
- Jargony (no unnecessary technical terms)
- Dismissive (validate current workarounds, show better way)

### Specific Examples

**Good:**
"You've been copy-pasting into Notion because it's the best option you've found. We built something better."

**Bad:**
"Stop wasting time with manual workflows! Our AI-powered solution revolutionizes knowledge capture!"

### Tone by Channel

**Landing page:** Professional, benefit-focused, empathetic
**Reddit comments:** Conversational, helpful, non-salesy
**Email:** Friendly, personal, value-focused
**Product description:** Clear, specific, outcome-oriented
