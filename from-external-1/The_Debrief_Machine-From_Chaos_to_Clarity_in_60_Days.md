# The Debrief Machine: From Chaos to Clarity in 60 Days

## The Origin Story

It started with a simple frustration: every AI conversation ended with knowledge loss. Important decisions, brilliant insights, and strategic planning would vanish into the void when clicking "New Chat."

**The specific request that started it all**: "I need something that captures EVERYTHING - no more losing track of decisions, no more forgetting what we discussed, no more starting from scratch."

In early 2025, this pain point sparked the creation of The Debrief Machine - what would evolve into a comprehensive AI conversation documentation system through rapid iteration and user-driven development.
